The Word “Fatigue”

A Vocabulary and Meaning

Etymology

From Old French fatigue, from Latin fatigare meaning "to tire or weary".

Definition

A fatigue is a word means extreamly tired, either physically or mentally.

Denotation

In its literal sense, intensifies refers to:

  • Being very tired
  • Physical exhaustion
Example (Denotation):
The players were fatigue after the long game.

Connotation

Beyond its literal meaning, fatigue often suggests:

  • Mental exhaustion
  • Emotional burnout
Example (Connotation):
She felt mentally fatigue after weeks of strees.
